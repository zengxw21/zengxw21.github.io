September Diary

from me to myself

18th

Two years ago,a girl told me that she thought the symbol of a girl turning to a woman is wearing heels,and asked me what is the symbol of a boy turning to a man.I remember that I couldn’t make an satisfying answer to her.I said cigarette and car,but she just shook her head and denied me.
And now,I think I’ve got the answer.It may be as simple as wearing a shirt with collar.At least this answer can satisfy me.

19th

Sometimes I feel myself sensitive and emotional.In my spare time, when I have nothing to do, I start to imagine what could happen in the future and I always think of the good side, and that drives me to face the problems bravely in life and make achievement.So I would rather say that I live in my imagination rather than the real world.

23th

I love riding.I like pedaling hard and make moves that are a little bit dangerous.Because when I get really fast,the surroundings become dangerous and make me nervous,and that kind of nervousness and the feeling that I might fall down and get injured remind me of my existence in this world.
It’s a special feeling when I hear my father telling me that I’m mature.I can’t help thinking it over and over again.Tonight I made a phone call to my parents,and I can feel that my father seem to be sorry for he could’t using his knowledge to give me advice and be my life mentor like he did when I was adolesent.As I grow up, I guadually find that my father actually does a really good job as a father.I used to listen to a song named these four walls，describing the respinsibility a man should take as a father,a son,a husband and a brother.The situation is even more common in China where most of the men would go through these stages.

25th

These days I find myself uneasy.Now I don’t know if I should go on without withdrawing database course because so many people quit because of its challenging course project.I’m not brave enough to face the truth and all the problems.”You have to pay for what you’ve done in the past.”That’s what I want to say to the old me.What goes around comes around.But if I’m back to one year ago,I am bound to make the same decision.That is the drawback of humanity.

26th

What is the meaning of taking a course with a giant project? To some extent the course project might fullfill your own CV and make yourself more competitive in the job market. This is true particularly when you only want to get a bachelor’s degree.However things will be different when you want to go further like getting a master degree.In the long run, you still get time for even more challenging and meaningful project and more internships.What’s more, a single course that are horrible can spoil the whole semester,which might do harm to your physical and mental health,especially when taking this course is not necessary for all the students.
Conclusion: I should withdraw the database course and make time for essential courses.
